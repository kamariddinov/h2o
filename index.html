About Us

We produce edible water

Cheap

Widespread

Ecologic

Wasteless

Edible water consists of water and sphere shaped cover which is edible material. You can purchase our production in any vending machine.

Our main purpose is to reduce the waste of plastic bottles which are used to keep clean water. The technology that allows to make water edible, thus the production of plastic bottle could be reduced to safe ecology

How we started

First we found an idea to reuse plastic bottles. But suddenly realised that replaceing them would be more ecologic. So we found an idea of edible water.

Process

We found investors, water and material suppliers. Daily we were producing more than 1000 bubbled waters. And placed them in vending machines.

Results

The product gathered popularity among people. The production increased 3 times. Now we still continue the production of edible water and saving the nature.
